Restored to Original Specifications

Each camera undergoes a rigorous refurbishment process using specialized equipment, ensuring it performs like new.

1. Detailed Inspection

Thorough check of all mechanical and electronic components.

2. Expert Servicing

Cleaning, lubrication, adjustment, and replacement of worn parts.

3. Rigorous Testing

Calibration and film testing to guarantee original performance standards.

Keepers of Analog History

Founded in 2002, Original Cameras was born from a passion for film photography and the timeless engineering of vintage cameras. We believe these mechanical marvels deserve a second life.

Our mission is to connect photographers with reliable, beautiful, and historically significant cameras that are ready for a new creative journey the moment they arrive at your door.
